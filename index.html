What is my2L?

my2L (myTool) is a collection of simple tools designed to help with daily decisions, organization, calculations, reminders, and everyday tasks.

🎲 what2Do

Can't decide? Input choices and let my2L choose for you.

👥 who2Pay

Split group expenses and easily know who owes who.

⏰ how2Wake

Solve 3 quick math problems to stop the alarm.

🍳 what2Eat

Find meals based on your available ingredients and get recipes.

💡 when2Bill

Track bills, payments, due dates, and monthly expenses.

📏 what2Length

Use your phone as a measurement reference and convert lengths.

🎒 what2Bring

Create smart checklists based on your situation.

🛒 what2Buy

Manage shopping lists and track purchased items.

🧠 what2Remember

Save notes, reminders, and important information.
