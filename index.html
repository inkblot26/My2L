What is my2L?

my2L (myTool) is a collection of simple tools designed to help with daily decisions, organization, calculations, reminders, and everyday tasks.

🎒 what2Bring

Create smart checklists for everyday activities and events.

🛒 what2Buy

Create shopping lists and track purchased items with ease.

🎲 what2Do

Can't decide? Let my2L randomly choose from your options.

📏 what2Measure

Measure objects using your phone and convert length units.

👥 who2Pay

Split expenses and instantly see who owes whom.

🧠 what2Remember

Save notes, reminders, and important information in one place.

⏰ how2Wake

Wake up by solving quick math problems to stop the alarm.

📸 what2Pic

Capture photos and save them with notes so you never forget important details.

🔳 what2QR

Scan QR codes from your camera or photos, and generate your own QR codes with ease.

🍳 what2Eat (not yet)

Find meals based on your available ingredients and get recipes.

💡 when2Bill (not yet)

Track bills, payments, due dates, and monthly expenses.
